## 📖 PRINT THE WISH BOOK

### Print Settings:
- **Paper Size**: A5 (148 × 210 mm)
- **Quality**: Best/High Quality
- **Color**: Full Color
- **Paper**: 120-160 GSM (thicker is better)
- **Duplex**: Single-sided

### Recommended Order:
1. Print `Melus_33_Wishes_Book.pdf`
2. Review pages (35 total)
3. Bind using:
   - Spiral binding (easiest - any print shop can do this)
   - Ring binding
   - Saddle stitch
   - Or punch holes and use ribbons

### Pro Tips:
- Use cream or off-white colored paper for vintage scrapbook feel
- Add blank pages between wishes for her to write notes
- Decorate with washi tape, stickers, or ribbons
- Consider laminating the cover page

## 🎯 How It Works

### For Melu:
1. **Opens wish book** → Sees codename (e.g., "Slow Sunday")
2. **Connects dots** → Numbered dots form QR code (1→2→3...)
3. **Scans QR** → Opens beautiful wish reveal page
4. **Clicks button** → WhatsApp message sent to you
5. **You receive** → "Hey Karthik! 💝 I'm activating Wish #01..."

### The Experience:
- **Tactile**: She physically creates each QR code by connecting dots
- **Interactive**: Scanning reveals the surprise
- **Romantic**: Beautiful web pages with your message
- **Memorable**: WhatsApp creates a record of each activated wish
